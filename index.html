Our Services

Now create you own pharmacy database and easily make medicines available to the world out there!

Pharmacists can easily upload their medicine datebase which will include their medical store name, location of the store, list of medicines and its availability and price. This database will be used by the site to show the availability of the specific medicine in the nearby stores which in turn will help people to easily get the medicine and they would have to no longer take the pain to go from store to store to get the medicines. Also pharmacists can start a delivery service if they want. This site will not only help the people to get medicine faster but also help pharmacists to grow in business.
